Welcome To International Islamic University Chittagong (IIUC) Alumni Assoication

Vision: To serve as a platform to connect the alumni of IIUC and continue the journey with active participation, mutual cooperation, professional development, social service and development in the life of hereafter.
Mission: To create a platform of existing alumnus and bridge the gap of communication. To act as a platform to support the alumni members in need, hunting career opportunity, business development and social service. To guide and channelize the efforts of the Alumni seeking better opportunities to learn and grow. Encourage and support alumnus to develop and spread a philanthropic and entrepreneurial mindset. To associate with the association in planning and execution of alumni events. To promote a lifelong relationship with values and pride.
